class koapy.utils.calendar.XKRXExchangeCalendar(start=start_default, end=end_default)[source]¶

Bases: exchange_calendars.exchange_calendar.ExchangeCalendar

Calendar for the Korea exchange, and the primary calendar for the country of South Korea.

Open Time: 9:00 AM, KST (Korean Standard Time) Close Time: 3:30 PM, KST (Korean Standard Time)

NOTE: Korea observes Standard Time year-round.

Due to the complexity around the Korean holidays, we are hardcoding a list of holidays covering 1986-2019, inclusive.

Regularly-Observed Holidays: - Seollal (New Year’s Day) - Independence Movement Day - Labor Day - Buddha’s Birthday - Memorial Day - Provincial Election Day - Liberation Day - Chuseok (Korean Thanksgiving) - National Foundation Day - Christmas Day - End of Year Holiday

NOTE: Hangeul Day became a national holiday in 2013 - Hangeul Proclamation Day
